### Test Case ID: TC_NAV_001

Title: Navigate between Shop, Men, Women, and Kids pages
User Story Reference: 3
Priority: High
Preconditions:

- The application navigation menu is available.
  Test Steps:

1. From the homepage, click the Shop link.
2. Click the Men link.
3. Click the Women link.
4. Click the Kids link.
   Expected Result:
5. Shop page loads and product listings are visible.
6. Men page loads and product listings are visible.
7. Women page loads and product listings are visible.
8. Kids page loads and product listings are visible.
   Each page loads successfully without errors.
   The correct category content is displayed.
   Notes / Assumptions:

- Validate that navigation returns to the expected category pages.
  Defect Opportunity:
- Broken category links or incorrect page content.
- Navigation causes unexpected redirects or errors.
